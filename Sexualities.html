Gay

(Referring to a male) being attracted to the male gender/aesthetic

Lesbian

(Referring to a female) being attracted to the female gender/aesthetic

Bisexual

(Referring to a person) being attracted to both the female and male gender/aesthetic

Pansexual

(Referring to a person) being attracted to all genders/aesthetics

Asexual

An umbrella term to describe a person that does not feel sexual attraction(For more information, look under Attractions)

Aromantic

An umbrella term to describe a person that does not feel romantic attraction(For more information, look under Attractions)

Non-Binary

A person that does not identify with male or female gender/pronouns and uses They/Them/Theirs pronouns

Demigender

A person that feels a partial connection to a particular gender identity, i.e., He/They, She/They, They/He, They/She, etc.

Demisexual

A person that feels sexual attraction towards someone only after the development of a close emotional bond

Polyamorous

The involvement in the practice of engaging in multiple romantic/sexual relationships with the consent of all people involved
