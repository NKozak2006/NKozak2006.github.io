Background

The lyrics imply that this song's theme is about finding love and how it may change your overall view. The song describes how the writer was looking for something and eventually discovered it in the shape of a romantic partner. The writer accepts the relationship with helping them get over their prior hurt and despair, describing them as a source of light and purification.
