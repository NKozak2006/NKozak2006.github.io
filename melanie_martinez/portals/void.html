Background

In “VOID,” Melanie describes the cruelty of her intrusive thoughts, taking experience from her hypnotherapy experiences. The song is meant to continue the concept of the afterlife, after the lead single “DEATH.” She kicks off the second track of her album with a fuse of pop-rock and punk–the perfect combination to compliment Melanie’s lively vocals.
