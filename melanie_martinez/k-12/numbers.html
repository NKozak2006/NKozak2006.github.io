Background

The heavy alternative electro-pop influenced track discusses the stress and issues music artists, including Melanie, go through in the musical industry. Melanie explained how it’s about “feeling underpaid and overworked in a system that treats you like you’re disposable;” the system in question obviously referring to the music industry. This song was initially released on an EP separate to the album.
