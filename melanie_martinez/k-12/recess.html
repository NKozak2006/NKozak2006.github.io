Background

“Recess” brings Melanie Martinez’s sophomore album K-12 to a sweet, fairytale close. In the album’s accompanying film, “Recess” plays during a nightmarish ball, where K-12 students, bedecked in their finery, are cursed to dance forever. Despite this grim outlook, “Recess” is a cheery anthem to letting go of one’s attachments and being brave enough to take a break when things get too hard.
