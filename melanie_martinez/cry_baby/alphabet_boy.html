Background

After being stuck on a carousel of a relationship, Cry Baby decides to break things off. This was the hardest song for Melanie to write due to the immense lyricism, such as alliteration that presses on the song’s theme of feeling belittled. The lyrics in verses 1 and 2 flow in alphabetical order from A to D.
