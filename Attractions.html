Sexual

Sexual attraction is the attraction or affinity to someone in a sex-related or lustful way

Sensual

Sensual attraction is the attraction or affinity to longing to be near physically with someone in a physical way, not sexually

Romantic

Romantic attraction is the attraction or affinity to wishing to date or engage in a romantic relationship with someone

Platonic

Platonic attraction is the attraction or affinity to wanting or getting joy out of friendships or friendly encounters

Aesthetic

Aesthetic attraction is the attraction or affinity to someone's appearance or style
